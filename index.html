Born and raised in Ottawa, Rory Charlton is proud to call the nation's capital home. She has always had a passion for real estate and helping others.

As a recipient of a two-year Algonquin College Business Marketing Diploma, Rory has a strong understanding of market trends and the importance of client satisfaction. After graduation, Rory acquired an abundance of industry knowledge and valuable work experience while working with accomplished agents as an unlicensed Administrative Assistant.

Rory’s warm and bright personality shines with her extensive customer service experience and ability to understand her clients’ needs. Her commitment is to make sure you and your family feel comfortable trusting her to take immense care of whichever real estate venture you may be embarking on, whether it’s buying or selling for yourself or as an investment.

Rory’s dedication to her passion will provide you and your family the highest level of service.
